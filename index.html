Split Expenses Fairly With Friends

Managing shared expenses has never been easier. Whether you are splitting dinner bills, sharing travel costs, managing household expenses, or tracking group spending, this application makes it simple and transparent for everyone involved.

Key features include real-time balance tracking, multiple split methods including equal and custom amounts, receipt attachments for expense documentation, payment recording and settlement tracking, group management with activity feeds, multi-currency support, and detailed expense reports with category breakdowns.

The app calculates who owes whom automatically, so you can focus on what matters. Create a group, add your expenses, and let the system handle the math. Settle debts with a single tap and keep everyone on the same page with real-time notifications and balance updates. Perfect for roommates, travel groups, couples, and any shared expense situation.

Built with privacy and security in mind, the platform uses row-level security to protect your financial data. Your information is never shared with third parties. Start splitting expenses fairly today and enjoy stress-free shared finances with the people you care about.
